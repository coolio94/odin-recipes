Pesto Pizza

Description

pizza made with italian ingredient pesto. This is one of the best pizzas you can ever taste.

Ingredients

  • 12 inch pre-baked pizza crust
  • half cup of pesto
  • one ripe tomato, chopped
  • half cup chopped green pepper
  • 2 ounce can chopped black olives
  • half small red onion
  • 4 ounce can artichoke hearts
  • one cup crumbled feta cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F.
  2. Spread pesto on pizza dust. Top with tomatoes, bell peppers, olives, red onions, artichoke hearts and feta cheese.
  3. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes, or until cheese is melted and browned.
